Privacy Policy & Cookies

1. Client-Side Data Storage

At Stackcraft Technologies, your privacy and security are elite priorities. 2. Conversational Voice AI

For applications utilizing our Voice Assistant feature, audio inputs are processed securely in real-time to execute your commands. We do not permanently record, store, or sell your voice queries to third-party advertising networks.

To protect your digital footprint, our authentication architecture employs a strict 3-day inactivity timer. If you do not access the platform within 72 hours, your session data is automatically wiped from your local device.

We utilize strictly necessary technical telemetry to maintain high-performance architectures, dynamic layouts, and dark-mode preferences. We do not participate in cross-site tracking or pixel harvesting.
